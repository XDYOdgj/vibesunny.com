Modern Bathroom Vanity Wall Light

This elegant 4-light wall sconce brings a contemporary feel to your bathroom, hallway, or bedroom with its sleek black metal base and four clear glass shades. Designed for both modern and rustic interiors, it blends style with functionality to complement any decor.

Its versatile installation allows you to direct the light either downward for overhead illumination or upward to accent your mirrors—an ideal choice for task lighting or creating a calming ambiance. The fixture features a dimmable switch, giving you control over brightness levels for a personalized lighting experience.

Easy to install with all hardware included, this vanity light is perfect for DIY enthusiasts. The open bottom design makes replacing bulbs a breeze, while the E26 bulb compatibility ensures you can use incandescent, CFL, LED, or halogen bulbs. Meeting UL safety standards, it’s a reliable and stylish addition to your home.
