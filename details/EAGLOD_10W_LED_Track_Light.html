EAGLOD 10W LED Track Light

Introducing a modern lighting solution that blends style and function. The EAGLOD 10W LED Track Light is designed to deliver smooth, adjustable lighting with a 3000K warm white color temperature. Whether you're lighting a store, gallery, or home, this light provides a soft, inviting glow that enhances any space.

With a dimmer that allows for 100% neutral dimming or a 10% flicker-free option, you can customize the brightness to match your mood or environment. The rotatable track head ensures a 360-degree range, making it easy to direct light precisely where it's needed. Perfect for accent lighting or task lighting, this unit is built for long-term use with excellent heat dissipation, lasting up to 50,000 hours.

The compact and sleek design of the EAGLOD 10W LED Track Light fits seamlessly in commercial and retail settings.
