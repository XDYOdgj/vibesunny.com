East Grove Home Amped Collection LED Track Head

Transform your space with the perfectly balanced lighting solution from East Grove. This sleek LED track head delivers warm, inviting illumination with a 3000K white light, creating a cozy and welcoming atmosphere in your home or office. Designed with energy efficiency in mind, it helps reduce your electricity bills without compromising on brightness or style.

Key Features

  • Durable die-cast aluminum build with a frosted TIR lens to minimize glare
  • 350-degree aiming with 90-degree vertical adjustment for precise lighting control
  • Hidden LED driver for clean, modern installation
  • Designed for use with H-Style 3-wire track systems for easy integration
  • Elegant Black finish that complements any interior design

Why Choose This LED Track Head?

Whether you're lighting a living room, a workspace, or an art gallery, the East Grove LED Track Head is the ideal choice. It combines modern design with practical performance, delivering 808 lumens of bright, even light from a 10W LED module. The 90% CRI rating ensures accurate color rendering, making it perfect for reading, working, or highlighting features in your environment. With 5% dimming compatibility, you can easily adjust the brightness to suit your mood or activity, all while enjoying a long-lasting and energy-efficient lighting solution.
